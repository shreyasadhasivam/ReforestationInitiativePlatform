About Us

The Reforestation Initiative is dedicated to restoring and preserving forests around the world. We believe in the importance of reforestation efforts to combat climate change and preserve biodiversity.

Our team consists of passionate individuals committed to making a positive impact on the environment.

    Udupi Highway Reforestation

    We are working to restore deforested areas in the Amazon rainforest by planting native tree species and implementing sustainable land management practices.

    Vajra Vana Regeneration

    In partnership with MAHE University, we are reforesting degraded landscapes in the MIT Campus to restore the native trees and improve ecosystem resilience. The area is around 1.5 acre and the forest will have around 1000 trees including local species from the Western Ghats.
